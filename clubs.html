Future Medical Practitioners

Future Medical Practitioners Club: Are you interested in the medical field and want service hour opportunities? If yes, the Future Medical Practitioners Club is for you! Being part of this club will give you the chance to learn about numerous professions in the medical field, attend meetings where members from the medical field will talk about their experiences, and help with the Blood Drive once a quarter. You will have numerous opportunities to gain service hours as well as collaborate with peers that share your same passion for medicine. Make sure to join our Remind by texting @cbhsfmp22 to 81010 to stay updated on anything going on in our club and! You may even DM us on Instagram @cbhsfmp.
